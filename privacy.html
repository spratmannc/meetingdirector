Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

Meeting Director includes a standard installation of Application Insights, and only collects information about software exceptions and crashes.


Information We Do Not Collect

Meeting Director accesses the current user's Picture, Video and Music Libraries for the purpose of projecting the media to connected speakers and monitors/projectors. It does not collect or store any information about that media, and at no time is the content or metadata of that media sent to any other computers on any network. Meeting Director does not collect or store any personally identifiable information, public or non-public.
